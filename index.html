Project Purpose

The Accounting Pathways Guide is being developed to help students better understand accounting career paths. The guide will provide clearer information about accounting roles, CPA pathways, industry opportunities, and the ways accounting connects with industry professionals.

Problem Statement

Many students may have a narrow view of accounting and may not fully understand the variety of career paths available within the profession. Students may also have questions or concerns about how AI could affect accounting careers. This project aims to address those concerns by giving students practical, accessible, and current information about the profession.

Target Audience

  • Current accounting students
  • Business students considering accounting
  • Students interested in CPA licensure
  • Advisors and faculty supporting career conversations

Project Goals

Increase Awareness

Help students understand the range of accounting careers.

Planned Deliverables

  • Published Accounting Pathways Guide
  • In-class presentations for accounting and business students
  • Industry speaker or professional perspective session
  • Optional one-on-one student conversations

Timeline

Summer 2026

Define project scope, gather resources, and draft the guide structure.

Fall 2026

Develop guide content and identify opportunities for classroom integration.

Spring 2027

Finalize the guide, deliver presentations, collect feedback, and evaluate project impact.

Measures of Success

  • The Accounting Pathways Guide is completed and shared with students
  • At least three student-facing presentations are delivered
  • At least one industry speaker or professional perspective is included
  • Student feedback shows improved understanding of accounting career options

Project Status

This project is currently in the planning and development stage. Next steps include continuing to develop the guide content, identifying key accounting career pathways to feature, and determining how the guide will be shared with students.
